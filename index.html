About Me

My name is Kim Dang, and I am currently a third-year Electrical Engineering student at University of California, Santa Barbara. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and innovation, and I'm driven by the potential of engineering solutions to reshape our world.

Beyond the academic realm, I enjoy biking, as it helps me appreciate the beauty of California's landscapes and further ignites my commitment to sustainability.

In this portfolio, you'll discover a blend of my technical expertise, leadership experiences, and the enthusiasm that fuels my journey in the ever-evolving field of engineering.
